Military doctor, inventor of the epidural anesthesia technique.

Epidural

A medical technique that delivers anesthetic near the spinal cord to block pain, commonly used during childbirth and surgeries.

Hipatia de Alejandria

Ancient mathematician and astronomer, pioneer of scientific thought and education.

An ancient astronomical instrument refined by Hypatia, used to measure the positions of stars and planets for navigation and timekeeping.

Leonardo Torres Quevedo

Engineer and inventor, creator of the first remote control and early automata.

Remote control

An early wireless device invented by Torres Quevedo to control machines remotely, considered a precursor to modern remote controls.
